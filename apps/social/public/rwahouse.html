What's Happening Inside RWA House

Real-world assets are moving on-chain faster than any other category in crypto — the RWA market crossed $382 billion as of April 2026, a $362 billion increase in a single year. Figure is where a lot of that movement is happening. YLDS, the first SEC-registered yield-bearing stablecoin, brings compliant yield on-chain. Hastra's PRIME token crossed $350M in TVL on Solana in under four months, without market makers or liquidity incentives. OPEN launched with FGRS as the first blockchain-native equity traded solely on blockchain rails.

RWA House is where we bring those conversations together. Over three days, Figure hosts founders, allocators, protocol builders, and the press to talk about what's working, what's next, and where the real institutional capital is headed.

Just the people actually building the thing.
