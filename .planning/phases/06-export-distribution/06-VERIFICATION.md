### Gaps Summary

Two gaps block full goal achievement:

**Gap 1 — DIST-01: No scheduled auto-delivery (blocking for requirement coverage)**
Success Criterion 2 in ROADMAP.md requires "Scheduled reports auto-deliver via email to configured recipients." Phase 6 builds only manual on-demand sending. This is the correct Phase 7 foundation — the email sender and tRPC mutation are ready for Phase 7 to trigger automatically. However, the success criterion is documented at Phase 6 scope, and REQUIREMENTS.md marks DIST-01 as "Complete" which is premature. Resolution options: (a) move DIST-01 and Success Criterion 2 to Phase 7 scope in ROADMAP/REQUIREMENTS, or (b) add minimal auto-trigger capability to Phase 6.

**Gap 2 — DIST-03: Per-schedule recipient configuration is Phase 7 scope**
DIST-03 requires recipients "per schedule." Phase 6 delivers localStorage defaults per user, which satisfies ad-hoc sending but not per-schedule configuration (which requires a Schedule model). Again, the REQUIREMENTS.md traceability marks this complete prematurely. Resolution: move to Phase 7 scope or confirm localStorage defaults are the intended interpretation.

**Note:** Both gaps stem from the ROADMAP including schedule-dependent success criteria in Phase 6 scope. The infrastructure built in Phase 6 (email sender, tRPC mutations, delivery logging) is the correct foundation — Phase 7 adds the scheduling layer that makes DIST-01 and DIST-03 fully satisfiable. The implementation quality of what was built is high with no stubs or anti-patterns.
